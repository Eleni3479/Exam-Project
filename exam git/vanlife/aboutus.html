ABOUT US

We are small starting company of young people who like travelling a lot and we want to share with you our experience of travelling through the vans. We want to give to our customers best memmories from journey and adventures from our vans.

WE CARE...

Because we know that there exist a lot of environmental problems in the world, and we are people who are trying live eco-friendly we also made our vans a little bit eco-friendly to our environment. Most of our vans have built in the roof their own solar panel which is used as a source of energy for your van on journey.

OUR TECHNOLOGY

Solar panels built in the roofs of our vans are used as a source of energy to vans. So you can use energy from that to cooking in your van or for lightning and heating. If you use energy from solar panel only for these things it will work for one week without charging it. But this energy from solar panel can be also used as motor energy instead of fuel for 200km. So if you are planning only short distance trip you can count on it that it wiill be eco-friendly!

WHY TO CHOOSE US?

The main idea that we have is to provide you not only with a car, but also with a place to stay on your short weekend trips, or a longer term road trip around the continent. These vans are not only cars, but living, creating, relaxing spaces that you can design for yourself with the help of ours. You, finding our van as your home, is what we are seeking for!
