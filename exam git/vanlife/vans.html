Find a perfect van!


VanLife can offer you three types of vans - The Hippie Van, The Retro Van, or The Explorer Van. Each of these vans are adjusted for traveling and living in it at the same time. You can customize it yourself, and start calling it home!

THE HIPPIE VAN




A unique caravan van made for nostalgic people. It has a kitchen inside and has a hightop, which makes every every-day task a lot easier. It has a pull out shower, a toilet inside. The Hippie Van is suitable for family of four.

THE RETRO VAN




The Retro Van is great for a solo traveler or a couple - it has place for two people inside. There is a kitchen, a toilet inside. It has a pull out shower. This van has a 120W solar panel, which means that you can always get some extra energy for charging your devices!

THE EXPLORER VAN




This van is a perfect choice for long road trips, no matter where you are. It has a heating system, a kitchen, a shower, and a toilet inside. It is suitable for four people. It also has a 120W solar panel!
